On June 3rd 2021, we partnered with DataPhilly and The DataLab to host a panel event with hiring managers in the fields of Data Science and Data Engineering

The event

Panelists shared their experiences of the hiring process and their recommendations for job-seekers. The discussion was expertly-moderated by Jessie Pluto, one of the R-Ladies Philly organizers. We asked the panel a mix of questions that attendees had submitted ahead of time and live-upvoted questions. The panel was followed by smaller conversations in breakout rooms, providing the opportunity for attendees to ask the panelists questions directly.

The panel

The panel included data professionals from a range of industries. We are grateful to our panelists, who were very generous with their insights and their time:

  • Anjali Bansal: Advanced Analytics Lead, Slalom

  • Robert Cheetham: CEO, Azavea

  • Johanna-Laina Fischer: Senior Data Scientist, Comcast EBI

  • Ryan Harrington: Lead Data Scientist, CompassRed

  • Dave O’ Toole: Vice President of Analytics, Consumer Behavior, and BI, Revzilla (Comoto Holdings)

  • Jason Rodriguez: Data Science Manager, GoPuff

  • Steve Ross: Sr. Manager Security Data Engineering, Elastic

  • Oodaye Shukla: Chief Data Analytics Officer, Eversana
